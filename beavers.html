1st Owlsmoor Beavers

Beavers are for boys and girls age 6 to 8. The colony meet on Tuesday evenings from 17:30 to 19:00. Beavers is all about fun, adventure and making new friends. At 1st Owlsmoor we plan a programme which includes hikes, camping, firelighting and cooking as well as activities such as crate stacking and archery. We also visit the Cubs and Scouts for trips and camps such as parent camp which is in September each year. We may not be able to offer your child a place immediately, however feel free to contact us and we can advise you of when a place may become available.

Full uniform is expected every week unless otherwise specified, details of where to buy uniform is below. Appropriate footwear is to be worn at all times. If you have skills that you want to share with us please let us know. For further information, please contact our Beaver leader.
