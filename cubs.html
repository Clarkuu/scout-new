1st Owlsmoor Cubs

Cubs are for boys and girls age 8 to 10Â½. The pack meet on Monday evenings from 18:00 to 19:30. Cubs is all about fun and adventure while learning life skills, responsibility and independence. At 1st Owlsmoor we plan a programme which includes hikes, camping, firelighting and cooking as well as activities such as climbing, dragon boating and archery. We also join the Beavers and Scouts for trips and camps such as parent camp which is in September each year.

We may not be able to offer your child a place immediately, however feel free to contact us and we can advise you of when a place may become available.

Full uniform is expected every week unless otherwise specified, details of where to buy uniform is below. Appropriate footwear is to be worn at all times.
